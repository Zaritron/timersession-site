Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Flexo Vibe ("we", "us") handles information when you use TimerSession.

Data We Collect

When you purchase or register, we collect your email address and payment information (processed securely by our payment provider). We do not have access to your payment card details.

Data We Do NOT Collect

TimerSession stores all client records, session data, and financial information locally on your computer only. We have no access to this data and it is never transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Email

  • To deliver your license key
  • To send important updates about the Software
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not sell or share your email with third parties.

License Verification

The Software periodically contacts our licensing server to verify your license key. Only the license key and an anonymized device identifier are transmitted — no personal or client data.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising.
