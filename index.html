FAQ

Common Questions

What operating systems does TimerSession support?+
TimerSession is a Windows desktop application. It runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11.
Do I need an internet connection?+
No. Timer, clients, and records work fully offline. Internet is only needed for Google Calendar sync and Telegram notifications.
How does the 30-day trial work?+
Download the app and get full access to all features for 30 days — no credit card required. After the trial you can use the free version or upgrade to Pro.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?+
Yes, cancel anytime. Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period. No hidden fees.
Which plan should I choose?+
Monthly ($9.99/mo) is best if you want flexibility with no commitment. Annual ($49/yr) is the most popular — you save over 59% and it works out to just $4.08/month. Lifetime ($99 once) is the best deal if you plan to use TimerSession for 2+ years — you pay once and never worry about billing again.
Is my client data private?+
Yes. All data is stored locally on your computer. We do not upload your client information to any server.
